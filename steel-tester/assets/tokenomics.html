Plain English

Why This Matters

How is the burn shown here?

The page reads the live on-chain supply, shows how much supply remains, and estimates how much has been permanently removed. It is meant to give people a clean burn snapshot at a glance.

Why keep fees visible?

Because trust matters. If a route costs 0.5%, we say 0.5%. If a stealth provider or outside service adds cost, that should be shown separately instead of hidden in the markup.

Why split revenue between treasury and chart support?

The treasury keeps the platform funded and growing. The burn route keeps removing supply from the market. The rewards vault route keeps building a SOL reward pool for holders without dumping more token emissions into the market.

How do staking multipliers work?

Rewards build continuously, not in one big random drop. The current live weighting is 0-6 days at 0.25x, 7-29 days at 1.0x, 30-89 days at 1.25x, 90-179 days at 1.5x, and 180+ days at 2.0x. Bigger bags help, but longer holds matter too.

Plain English

Why This Matters

Why measure burns from 1B?

Because it gives people a dead-simple benchmark. If the token started at 1,000,000,000 supply, then every token permanently removed reduces that number. The remaining supply tells the story at a glance.

Why keep fees visible?

Because trust matters. If a route costs 0.5%, we say 0.5%. If a stealth provider or outside service adds cost, that should be shown separately instead of hidden in the markup.

Why split revenue between treasury and chart support?

The treasury keeps the platform funded and growing. The other half feeds the burn-side flow so platform revenue keeps creating visible on-chart support instead of disappearing into pure operations.

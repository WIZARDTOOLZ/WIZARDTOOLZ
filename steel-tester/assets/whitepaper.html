WIZARD TOOLZ

WIZARD TOOLZ is a Telegram-first Solana platform for launches, trading, growth, automation, and holder utility. The goal is not to be one more single-purpose bot. The goal is to connect the full operating stack: launch tools, multi-wallet execution, sniper flows, volume tools, burn support, wallet tracking, and the holder-rewards layer that turns real platform activity into visible ecosystem value.

1. Mission

Most Solana teams still stitch together launches, sniping, wallet management, reactions, chart support, and monitoring through scattered bots and separate service providers. WIZARD TOOLZ is designed to unify that into one product surface with one brand, one workflow, and one economics model.

2. Product Surface

The current stack includes Reaction Booster, Volume Booster, Buy / Sell, Burn Agent, Holder Booster, FOMO Booster, Magic Sell, Bundle, Launch + Buy, Sniper Wizard, Vanity Wallet, Vision, Wallet Tracker, X Followers, Engagement, Subs + Accounts, Spaces Booster, and Resizer.

3. Core Idea

WIZARD TOOLZ is built around execution and coordination. Wallet generation, funding, warming, launch actions, post-launch trading, reactions, and monitoring should fit together instead of forcing users to jump between separate tools.

4. Token Role

$WIZTOOLZ is the ecosystem token. It is tied to burn visibility, future holder rewards, staking utility, launchpad expansion, and the broader platform story. The token is not meant to exist without product support; the product is meant to keep feeding value back into the token layer.

5. Economics Model

Net platform profit is routed through a fixed split:

  • Treasury50%
  • Buyback + Burn25%
  • SOL Rewards Vault25%

This model is meant to keep the business funded, keep chart support visible, and keep a growing SOL rewards pool available for holder utility without relying on endless token emissions.

6. Burn System

Burn visibility matters because it makes the support loop obvious. The burn meter reads live on-chain supply and estimates how much supply has been permanently removed from circulation. It is not meant to be vague marketing. It is meant to show the effect of the system directly.

7. Rewards Vault

Holder rewards are intended to be paid in SOL from the rewards vault, not through constant token inflation. That makes the holder layer cleaner, more sustainable, and easier to explain. Bigger size and longer hold time create larger reward weight.

8. Staking Direction

The staking model is designed as a holder-tracking and rewards-claim layer. Claims are manual through the bot. Minimum claim thresholds prevent dust abuse. Time-based weighting rewards conviction over quick in-and-out farming.

9. Launch Tools

Launch + Buy and Sniper Wizard are built around speed, warmed wallets, and multi-wallet coordination. The goal is to let serious teams and serious traders prepare before the live moment instead of scrambling at the exact second a launch occurs.

10. Growth Layer

Growth is part of the platform, not an afterthought. Reaction Booster supports Dex reactions directly through the bot, while the broader service layer covers visibility, engagement, follower packages, and support-driven campaigns that help projects look active, credible, and coordinated from day one.

11. Roadmap

  • Current phaseTelegram trading, launch, growth, and automation stack
  • Next phaseStaking, SOL rewards visibility, stronger holder utility
  • ExpansionIntegrated communities, live rooms, launchpad infrastructure
  • Longer termFull token surface with creator context, discovery, and post-grad trading

12. Positioning

WIZARD TOOLZ is meant to feel like infrastructure, not a toy. The platform is built for operators who care about speed, control, coordination, and visible value loops. The whitepaper is intentionally practical: real tools, real flows, real economics, and a clear path from bot utility to a much bigger ecosystem.
